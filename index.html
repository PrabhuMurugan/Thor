No, Thor being immortal is a very common misconception. He as well as any other Asgardian do die eventually. Loki stated in 2013’s Thor: The Dark World that Asgardians do have extended lifespans of a few thousand years, but that’s still far from actual immortality. According the marvel wiki, even in the comics, Thor isn’t immortal, but rather have a extended lifespan like in the MCU. He ages so slowly that humans might perceive it as immortality. In addition, Thor consumes the Golden Apples cultivated by Idunn, which allows Thor to live longer. In the end, Thor himself is not immortal. He has an extended lifespan and relies on the Golden Apples to make himself live longer.
